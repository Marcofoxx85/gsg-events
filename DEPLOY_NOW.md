# Deploy GSG Events in 5 Minutes

**Your Railway Project**: https://railway.com/project/d4ef56d3-e5bc-4b03-9e35-8b6a27cbf728

## Step 1: Add PostgreSQL (30 seconds)
1. Click **"+ New"**
2. Click **"Database"**
3. Click **"Add PostgreSQL"**
4. Done! âœ…

## Step 2: Deploy Backend (2 minutes)
1. Click **"+ New"**
2. Click **"GitHub Repo"**
3. Select **"Marcofoxx85/gsg-events"**
4. In the popup, find **"Root Directory"** and click **"Add"**
5. Type: `/backend` (yes, with the slash)
6. Click **"Deploy"**
7. Wait for it to build (~1 minute)
8. Done! âœ…

## Step 3: Deploy Frontend (2 minutes)  
1. Click **"+ New"**
2. Click **"GitHub Repo"**
3. Select **"Marcofoxx85/gsg-events"** again
4. In the popup, find **"Root Directory"** and click **"Add"**
5. Type: `/frontend` (yes, with the slash)
6. Click **"Deploy"**
7. Wait for it to build (~1 minute)
8. Done! âœ…

## Step 4: Get Your URLs (30 seconds)
**For Backend:**
1. Click the backend service card
2. Click **"Settings"** tab
3. Scroll to **"Networking"**
4. Click **"Generate Domain"**
5. Copy the URL

**For Frontend:**
1. Click the frontend service card  
2. Click **"Settings"** tab
3. Scroll to **"Networking"**
4. Click **"Generate Domain"**
5. Copy the URL
